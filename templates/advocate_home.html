Legal Advisor

A Legal Advisor is a person who provides legal advice, often in an official capacity. Government officials that qualify as lawyers (jurist) in certain countries can get the title Legal Adviser.

Welcome to our website

The job of a Legal Advisor is to provide legal counsel to a client including a large corporation or an organization. Sometimes, a Legal Advisor is referred to as “in-house counsel”, “in-house lawyers” and “corporate counsel. ... Their educational path is similar to that of a lawyer.
